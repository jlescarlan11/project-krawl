## Summary / Overview

This document provides comprehensive user persona profiles for Krawl, a community-driven Progressive Web App that maps authentic Filipino culture through user-curated points of interest ("Gems") and guided trails ("Krawls"). Each persona profile describes a typical user type, including relevant demographics, goals, behaviors, pain points, and technology preferences to help guide design and development decisions.

**Purpose:** To establish clear, detailed user personas that inform all design, development, and product decisions for Krawl, ensuring the platform meets the needs of its diverse user base while maintaining focus on authentic Filipino cultural discovery in Cebu City.

## Persona Overview

Krawl serves three primary user segments, each with distinct needs, behaviors, and goals:

### Primary Users (Core Audience)
- **Young Filipino Adults (18-35) in Cebu City** - The main user base who discover, create, and curate cultural content
- **Community Contributors** - Active users who create Gems and Krawls, vouch for content, and maintain quality
- **Students & Researchers** - Users who explore cultural heritage for academic or personal learning

### Secondary Users (Discovery & Exploration)
- **Domestic Travelers** - Filipinos from other cities visiting Cebu
- **International Visitors** - Foreign tourists seeking authentic cultural experiences

### Tertiary Users (Future Revenue Model)
- **Local Business Owners** - Cultural establishments that may participate in the "Claim Your Gem" freemium model

## Technology Preferences

### Common Technology Preferences Across All Personas

All user personas share common technology preferences that inform Krawl's technical decisions:

#### Authentication

- **Social Login Only:** All personas prefer social login (Google) for quick, seamless access
- **No Email/Password:** Avoid traditional email/password authentication
- **Quick Onboarding:** Instant access without lengthy registration processes

#### Cost Considerations

- **Free Services:** All personas require free access to core features
- **Generous Free Tiers:** Services must offer reasonable free usage limits
- **No Paywalls:** Avoid premium features that block core functionality
- **Student-Friendly:** Budget-conscious approach suitable for students

#### Platform Preferences

- **Mobile-First:** All personas primarily use smartphones
- **PWA Support:** Progressive Web App functionality for app-like experience
- **Cross-Platform:** Works on iOS and Android
- **Offline Capability:** Essential for areas with poor connectivity

#### Maintenance & Updates

- **Well-Maintained:** All personas expect current, regularly updated tools
- **Active Development:** Services must be actively maintained
- **Community Support:** Active community and support resources
- **Documentation:** Clear documentation and tutorials

#### Scalability & Adaptability

- **Scalable:** Tools must scale with project growth
- **Agile-Friendly:** Support agile development principles
- **Adaptable:** Easy to modify and extend
- **Future-Proof:** Technologies that will remain relevant

## Design Implications

### Universal Design Principles

Based on all user personas, Krawl should follow these universal design principles:

#### 1. **Quick & Seamless Onboarding**

- **Social Login:** Google login for instant access (no email/password)
- **Minimal Friction:** Reduce onboarding steps to absolute minimum
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Show features gradually, not all at once
- **Tutorial:** Optional, skippable tutorial for new users

#### 2. **Mobile-First Design**

- **Touch-Optimized:** Large touch targets, thumb-friendly navigation
- **Responsive:** Works on all screen sizes, but optimized for mobile
- **Performance:** Fast loading, smooth animations
- **PWA:** App-like experience with offline capability

#### 3. **Offline-First Architecture**

- **Downloadable Krawls:** Users can download Krawls for offline use
- **Offline Maps:** Map tiles cached for offline access
- **Sync When Online:** Content syncs when connectivity is available
- **Graceful Degradation:** App works with poor connectivity

#### 4. **Free & Accessible**

- **No Paywalls:** All core features available for free
- **No Premium Tiers:** Avoid premium features that block functionality
- **Generous Limits:** Free tier limits are generous for typical usage
- **Transparent:** Clear communication about free tier limits

#### 5. **Cultural Authenticity**

- **Authentic Content:** Emphasize genuine, non-commercial experiences
- **Community Verification:** Vouching and rating systems for quality
- **Cultural Context:** Rich cultural explanations and significance
- **Respectful Representation:** Honor Filipino culture respectfully

#### 6. **Simple & Intuitive**

- **Clear Navigation:** Intuitive navigation, easy to understand
- **Minimal Learning Curve:** Users can start using immediately
- **Help & Support:** Clear documentation and support resources
- **Error Prevention:** Prevent errors through good design

#### 7. **Community-Driven**

- **Easy Contribution:** Simple Gem and Krawl creation flows
- **Recognition:** Profile showcasing contributions and reputation
- **Quality Control:** Vouching, rating, and reporting systems
- **Community Engagement:** Features that foster community connection
