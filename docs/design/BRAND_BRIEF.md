## Mission Statement

**To empower communities to discover, share, and preserve authentic Filipino culture by creating a living, community-driven map of cultural experiences that connects people with the genuine heart of their heritage.**

Krawl exists to bridge the gap between digital discovery and authentic cultural experiences. We believe that the most meaningful cultural discoveries come from the community itself‚Äîfrom locals who know the hidden gems, the stories behind the places, and the trails that connect them. Our mission is to provide a platform where Filipino culture can be mapped, shared, and explored in a way that honors authenticity, community knowledge, and cultural preservation.

---

## Vision Statement

**To become the definitive platform for discovering authentic Filipino culture, where every community member can contribute to and explore a living map of cultural heritage that grows richer with each shared experience.**

Our vision extends beyond Cebu City. We envision Krawl as a platform that will eventually map authentic Filipino culture across the Philippines, creating a comprehensive digital archive of cultural experiences that:

- **Preserves Cultural Heritage:** Documents authentic cultural locations and experiences for future generations
- **Empowers Communities:** Gives local communities the tools to share their cultural knowledge
- **Connects People:** Brings together locals and visitors through shared cultural experiences
- **Fosters Discovery:** Makes authentic cultural discovery accessible, engaging, and meaningful
- **Builds Bridges:** Connects the digital world with real-world cultural experiences

## Brand Story

Krawl was born from a simple observation: the most authentic cultural experiences are often hidden, known only to locals who have lived and breathed the culture. While commercial platforms focus on tourist attractions and paid placements, the genuine heart of Filipino culture‚Äîthe neighborhood eateries, the local artisans, the community gathering places, the historical sites with untold stories‚Äîremains unmapped and undiscovered.

**The Journey Begins:**

The idea for Krawl emerged from conversations with young Filipinos in Cebu City who expressed frustration at the difficulty of finding authentic cultural experiences. They wanted to discover the real Cebu‚Äîthe places where locals gather, the spots with cultural significance, the trails that tell stories of Filipino heritage. But existing platforms were either too commercial, too generic, or too focused on tourist attractions.

**The Solution:**

Krawl was conceived as a community-driven platform where locals could map their cultural knowledge, creating "Gems" (points of interest) and "Krawls" (guided trails) that connect these cultural experiences. Unlike commercial platforms, Krawl prioritizes authenticity over advertising, community knowledge over corporate content, and cultural preservation over commercialization.

**The Name:**

"Krawl" is a play on the word "crawl"‚Äîsuggesting a slow, deliberate exploration of culture, taking time to discover and appreciate each cultural gem. The name also evokes the idea of a trail or path, connecting multiple cultural points in a meaningful journey.

**The Vision:**

Starting in Cebu City, Krawl aims to become the living map of Filipino culture‚Äîa platform that grows richer with each community contribution, preserving cultural heritage while making authentic discovery accessible to all.

**The Promise:**

Krawl promises to be the platform where authentic Filipino culture is discovered, shared, and preserved‚Äîby the community, for the community, and for future generations.

## Brand Values

### 1. **Authenticity**
We prioritize genuine, non-commercial cultural experiences over tourist attractions. Authenticity is at the core of everything we do‚Äîfrom the content we feature to the community we build.

### 2. **Community-Driven**
Krawl is built by and for the community. We believe that the best cultural knowledge comes from locals who live and breathe the culture. Community contributions, vouching, and quality control are central to our platform.

### 3. **Cultural Preservation**
We are committed to preserving Filipino cultural heritage by documenting authentic cultural locations and experiences for future generations. Every Gem and Krawl contributes to a living archive of Filipino culture.

### 4. **Accessibility**
We believe authentic cultural discovery should be accessible to everyone. Our platform is free to use, works offline, and is designed to be inclusive and welcoming to all community members.

### 5. **Innovation**
We embrace technology to enhance cultural discovery while respecting tradition. Our offline-first approach, location-aware guidance, and community-driven quality control represent innovative solutions to cultural mapping.

### 6. **Respect**
We respect local culture, community knowledge, and the authentic experiences that make Filipino culture unique. We prioritize cultural sensitivity and community values in all our decisions.

### 7. **Transparency**
We are transparent about our mission, our values, and our approach. We believe in honest communication with our community and clear expectations about our platform's purpose and limitations.

## Tone of Voice

Krawl's tone of voice should be **authentic, welcoming, and culturally respectful** while remaining **clear, accessible, and engaging**. Our communication should reflect our values of community, authenticity, and cultural preservation.

### **Primary Tone Characteristics:**

1. **Authentic & Genuine**
   - Use real, honest language‚Äîno marketing fluff or corporate speak
   - Speak from the heart of the community
   - Avoid over-promising or exaggerating
   - Example: "Discover authentic Filipino culture" not "Experience the ultimate cultural journey"

2. **Welcoming & Inclusive**
   - Warm, friendly, and approachable
   - Invites participation and contribution
   - Makes everyone feel like part of the community
   - Example: "Join us in mapping Filipino culture" not "Sign up now to access premium features"

3. **Culturally Respectful**
   - Honors Filipino culture and traditions
   - Uses appropriate cultural references
   - Respects local knowledge and community wisdom
   - Example: "Share your cultural knowledge" not "Upload your content"

4. **Clear & Accessible**
   - Simple, straightforward language
   - Avoids jargon and technical complexity
   - Explains concepts clearly
   - Example: "Create a trail connecting cultural locations" not "Leverage our trail curation interface"

5. **Engaging & Inspiring**
   - Encourages exploration and discovery
   - Inspires cultural curiosity
   - Makes cultural discovery exciting
   - Example: "Follow the trail to discover hidden cultural gems" not "Navigate to waypoints"

### **Tone Variations by Context:**

**User-Facing Content (App, Website):**
- Warm, inviting, and encouraging
- Clear instructions and helpful guidance
- Celebrates community contributions

**Marketing & Social Media:**
- Energetic and discovery-focused
- Highlights authentic experiences
- Showcases community stories

**Documentation & Support:**
- Clear, helpful, and instructional
- Patient and understanding
- Focuses on user success

**Community Communication:**
- Collaborative and appreciative
- Values contributions
- Fosters community spirit

### **Language Guidelines:**

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Use "we" and "our community" to emphasize collaboration
- ‚úÖ Use active voice ("Discover gems" not "Gems can be discovered")
- ‚úÖ Use inclusive language ("everyone" not "users")
- ‚úÖ Celebrate Filipino culture respectfully
- ‚úÖ Use clear, simple sentences
- ‚úÖ Show appreciation for community contributions

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Use corporate jargon or marketing buzzwords
- ‚ùå Over-promise or exaggerate features
- ‚ùå Use exclusionary or divisive language
- ‚ùå Disrespect or trivialize cultural heritage
- ‚ùå Use overly technical language
- ‚ùå Make assumptions about cultural knowledge

### **Voice Examples:**

**Good:**
- "Join our community in mapping authentic Filipino culture. Every Gem you create helps preserve our heritage for future generations."

## Unique Selling Points (USPs)

### 1. **Krawl Mode: Location-Aware Guided Experience**
**What it is:** Interactive, real-time guided experience that helps users follow a Krawl with turn-by-turn directions, arrival detection, and progress tracking.

**Why it's unique:** Unlike static maps or generic navigation apps, Krawl Mode provides a purpose-built experience for cultural exploration, combining location awareness with cultural context and community knowledge.

**Benefit:** Users can immerse themselves in cultural discovery without constantly checking maps or worrying about navigation, allowing them to focus on the cultural experience itself.

### 2. **Offline-First Architecture**
**What it is:** Downloadable Krawls that work completely offline, including map tiles, Gem data, and navigation guidance.

**Why it's unique:** Many cultural locations have inconsistent connectivity. Krawl's offline-first approach ensures users can explore cultural trails even in areas with poor internet access.

**Benefit:** Users can confidently explore cultural locations without worrying about connectivity, making authentic cultural discovery accessible even in remote or low-connectivity areas.

### 3. **Community-Curated Content with Quality Control**
**What it is:** All content is created by community members, with quality control through vouching, rating, and reporting systems.

**Why it's unique:** Unlike commercial platforms driven by paid placements or algorithm-based recommendations, Krawl's content is entirely community-driven, ensuring authenticity and cultural relevance.

**Benefit:** Users can trust that the content they discover is authentic, verified by the community, and free from commercial bias.

### 4. **Hyperlocal Focus on Authentic Filipino Culture**
**What it is:** Exclusive focus on authentic, non-commercial Filipino cultural experiences, starting with Cebu City.

**Why it's unique:** While other platforms mix commercial attractions with cultural content, Krawl is dedicated exclusively to authentic cultural experiences, ensuring every Gem and Krawl represents genuine Filipino culture.

**Benefit:** Users can discover authentic cultural experiences without sifting through commercial content or tourist traps.

### 5. **Structured Cultural Trails (Krawls)**
**What it is:** Guided trails that connect multiple cultural Gems in a meaningful sequence, with estimated duration, difficulty, and cultural themes.

**Why it's unique:** Krawls provide structured cultural exploration, allowing users to follow curated trails that tell stories and connect cultural experiences in meaningful ways.

**Benefit:** Users can explore multiple cultural locations in a cohesive journey, rather than randomly discovering individual points of interest.

### 6. **Free and Accessible Platform**
**What it is:** Completely free to use, with no premium features or paywalls, built using free-tier services suitable for student projects.

**Why it's unique:** Krawl is committed to making authentic cultural discovery accessible to everyone, regardless of financial means.

**Benefit:** Users can access all features without cost barriers, making cultural discovery truly accessible to the community.

### 7. **Cultural Preservation Mission**
**What it is:** Every Gem and Krawl contributes to a living archive of Filipino cultural heritage, preserving authentic experiences for future generations.

**Why it's unique:** Krawl goes beyond discovery to actively preserve cultural heritage through community documentation.

**Benefit:** Users contribute to cultural preservation while discovering authentic experiences, creating a sense of purpose and community contribution.

## Visual Identity

### **Logo**

Krawl's logo represents a **breadcrumb trail** connecting cultural "Gems" (points of interest). The design symbolizes:
- **Journey & Discovery** - The trail path represents exploration
- **Connection** - Gems connected by a path show cultural continuity
- **Community** - Multiple points represent community-curated content
- **Movement** - The curved path suggests dynamic exploration

**Logo Variations:**
- Full Color (Primary Green `#2D7A3E`, Accent Orange `#FF6B35`, Warm Yellow `#F7B801`)
- Black & White
- White (for dark backgrounds)
- Monochrome Green
- Favicon

For complete logo guidelines, see [logo/LOGO_GUIDELINES.md](./logo/LOGO_GUIDELINES.md).

**Primary Colors:**
- **Primary Green:** `#2D7A3E` (RGB: 45, 122, 62) - Represents growth, nature, and Filipino heritage
- **Accent Orange:** `#FF6B35` (RGB: 255, 107, 53) - Represents energy, warmth, and cultural vibrancy

- Accent Orange: Highlights, important actions, cultural vibrancy
- Warm Yellow: Discovery indicators, positive feedback, cultural richness
