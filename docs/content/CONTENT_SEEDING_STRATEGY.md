# Content Seeding Strategy: Krawl MVP

## Summary / Overview

This document provides comprehensive guidelines for creating and seeding initial content for the Krawl MVP launch. The strategy ensures high-quality, authentic cultural content is distributed across Cebu City districts, meeting the launch goal of 100+ Gems and 10+ Krawls.

**Content Goals:**
- 100+ Gems across Cebu City districts
- 10+ Krawls connecting Gems in meaningful cultural trails
- High-quality, authentic Filipino cultural experiences
- Geographic distribution across key districts
- All content within Cebu City boundaries

## Content Creation Guidelines

### Gem Creation Standards

#### Required Information
- **Name:** Clear, descriptive name (max 100 characters)
- **Location:** Precise coordinates within Cebu City boundaries
- **Category:** Appropriate cultural category
- **Description:** Minimum 50 characters, maximum 500 characters
  - Must explain cultural significance
  - Should be authentic and non-commercial
  - Include relevant historical or cultural context

#### Recommended Information
- **Photos:** 1-5 high-quality photos (see Photo Requirements)
- **Cultural Significance Notes:** Additional context (max 300 characters)
- **Tags:** Up to 5 relevant keywords for discoverability

#### SEO Considerations

When creating Gems, consider SEO best practices to improve discoverability:

- **Keyword Integration:** Naturally include relevant keywords in Gem names and descriptions (e.g., location names, cultural terms, category keywords)
- **SEO-Friendly Naming:** Use descriptive, keyword-rich names that clearly identify the location and its cultural significance
- **Natural Language:** Keywords should flow naturally within descriptions—avoid keyword stuffing
- **Local Keywords:** Include Cebu City and district names where relevant
- **Long-Tail Keywords:** Consider how users might search for this cultural experience

For comprehensive SEO guidelines, keyword strategy, and optimization recommendations, see [SEO_PLAN_AND_KEYWORD_STRATEGY.md](./SEO_PLAN_AND_KEYWORD_STRATEGY.md).

#### Content Focus
- **Authentic Filipino Culture:** Traditional practices, local customs, historical sites
- **Non-Commercial:** Avoid purely commercial establishments unless culturally significant
- **Community-Driven:** Focus on experiences valued by local community
- **Accessible:** Consider accessibility and inclusivity

### Brand Alignment

All content descriptions, Gem names, and Krawl narratives should align with Krawl's brand tone of voice (see [BRAND_BRIEF.md](./BRAND_BRIEF.md)). Key considerations:

- **Authentic & Genuine:** Use real, honest language—no marketing fluff or corporate speak
- **Welcoming & Inclusive:** Warm, friendly, and approachable tone that invites participation
- **Culturally Respectful:** Honor Filipino culture and traditions, use appropriate cultural references
- **Clear & Accessible:** Simple, straightforward language that avoids jargon
- **Engaging & Inspiring:** Encourage exploration and discovery, inspire cultural curiosity

For detailed cultural sensitivity guidelines, see [BRAND_BRIEF.md](./BRAND_BRIEF.md#appendix-b-cultural-sensitivity-guidelines).

**Note:** SEO optimization should align with brand tone of voice. All keyword integration must maintain authenticity and cultural respect. See [SEO_PLAN_AND_KEYWORD_STRATEGY.md](./SEO_PLAN_AND_KEYWORD_STRATEGY.md) for SEO guidelines that align with brand values.

### Krawl Creation Standards

#### Required Information
- **Name:** Descriptive trail name (max 100 characters)
- **Description:** Comprehensive description (minimum 50 characters)
- **Gems:** Minimum 2 Gems, all within Cebu City
- **Route:** Logical sequence connecting Gems
- **Estimated Duration:** Realistic time estimate
- **Difficulty Level:** Appropriate difficulty rating

#### Recommended Information
- **Category/Theme:** Cultural theme or focus
- **Cover Image:** High-quality cover image
- **Route Optimization:** Efficient route connecting Gems

## Quality Standards

### Content Quality Criteria

1. **Authenticity**
   - Content must represent genuine Filipino cultural experiences
   - Avoid generic or tourist-trap locations
   - Prioritize local knowledge and community value

2. **Accuracy**
   - Location coordinates must be precise
   - Descriptions must be factually accurate
   - Cultural information must be verified

3. **Completeness**
   - All required fields must be filled
   - Descriptions should be informative and engaging
   - Photos should be relevant and high-quality

4. **Relevance**
   - Content must align with Krawl's mission
   - Must be culturally significant
   - Should appeal to target audience (young Filipino adults 18-35)

5. **Boundary Compliance**
   - All locations must be within Cebu City boundaries
   - No content outside launch area
   - Geographic validation required

### Quality Checklist

Before submitting content, verify:
- [ ] Location is within Cebu City boundaries
- [ ] All required fields are completed
- [ ] Description meets minimum length (50 characters)
- [ ] Description explains cultural significance
- [ ] At least one photo is included (recommended)
- [ ] Category is appropriate
- [ ] Content is authentic and non-commercial
- [ ] Information is accurate
- [ ] Spelling and grammar are correct

## Geographic Distribution

### Target Districts

Content should be distributed across key Cebu City districts:

1. **Downtown Cebu (Poblacion)**
   - Historical sites
   - Cultural landmarks
   - Traditional markets
   - Target: 20-25 Gems, 2-3 Krawls

2. **Colon Street Area**
   - Oldest street in Philippines
   - Historical significance
   - Cultural heritage sites
   - Target: 15-20 Gems, 1-2 Krawls

3. **Lahug Area**
   - Modern cultural spaces
   - Art galleries
   - Cultural centers
   - Target: 15-20 Gems, 1-2 Krawls

4. **Mabolo Area**
   - Local markets
   - Community spaces
   - Cultural practices
   - Target: 10-15 Gems, 1 Krawl

5. **Banilad Area**
   - Cultural institutions
   - Community centers
   - Local traditions
   - Target: 10-15 Gems, 1 Krawl

6. **Other Districts**
   - Additional areas within Cebu City
   - Target: 25-30 Gems, 2-3 Krawls

### Distribution Strategy

- **Even Coverage:** Avoid clustering all content in one area
- **Accessibility:** Include content accessible by public transport
- **Diversity:** Mix of historical, cultural, and community-focused locations
- **Balance:** Mix of indoor and outdoor experiences

## Content Validation Process

### Pre-Submission Validation

1. **Location Validation**
   - Verify coordinates are within Cebu City boundaries
   - Check location accuracy using map tools
   - Ensure location is accessible

2. **Content Review**
   - Review description for completeness
   - Verify cultural significance
   - Check for accuracy and authenticity
   - Ensure non-commercial focus

3. **Photo Validation**
   - Verify photos are relevant
   - Check photo quality (see Photo Requirements)
   - Ensure photos are appropriate

### Post-Submission Validation

1. **Automated Checks**
   - Boundary validation (automatic)
   - Required field validation
   - Character count validation

2. **Manual Review**
   - Content quality assessment
   - Cultural authenticity verification
   - Accuracy check
   - Photo quality review

3. **Community Review**
   - Vouching system (post-launch)
   - Rating system (post-launch)
   - Reporting system for issues

## Photo Requirements

### Technical Requirements

- **Format:** JPEG or PNG
- **Size:** Minimum 800x600 pixels
- **File Size:** Maximum 5MB per image
- **Quantity:** 1-5 photos per Gem
- **Quality:** High resolution, clear images

### Content Requirements

- **Relevance:** Photos must be relevant to the Gem
- **Authenticity:** Photos should represent actual location/experience
- **Quality:** Clear, well-lit, in-focus images
- **Appropriateness:** No inappropriate or offensive content
- **Ownership:** Must have permission to use photos

### Photo Guidelines

1. **Primary Photo:** Best representative image (used as thumbnail)
2. **Additional Photos:** Show different angles or aspects
3. **Cultural Context:** Include photos that show cultural significance
4. **People:** If including people, ensure consent
5. **Signage:** Include photos of relevant signs or markers when helpful

### Photo Optimization

- Compress images before upload (Cloudinary will optimize further)
- Use appropriate aspect ratios
- Ensure good lighting
- Remove unnecessary elements

## Review and Approval Process

### Review Workflow

1. **Content Creation**
   - Creator creates Gem/Krawl using app interface
   - Content saved as draft
   - Creator reviews and edits

2. **Pre-Submission Review**
   - Creator performs self-review using quality checklist
   - Content validated against requirements
   - Photos reviewed and optimized

3. **Submission**
   - Content submitted for review
   - Automated validation runs
   - Content enters review queue

4. **Review Process**
   - Content reviewed by founding team/curators
   - Quality assessment performed
   - Cultural authenticity verified
   - Accuracy checked

5. **Approval/Rejection**
   - Approved content published immediately
   - Rejected content returned with feedback
   - Creator can revise and resubmit

### Review Criteria

**Approval Requirements:**
- All required fields completed
- Location within Cebu City boundaries
- Content meets quality standards
- Photos meet requirements
- Cultural significance clear
- Non-commercial focus maintained

**Rejection Reasons:**
- Location outside Cebu City
- Missing required information
- Poor quality content
- Commercial focus
- Inaccurate information
- Inappropriate content

### Feedback Process

- Rejected content includes specific feedback
- Creator can address feedback and resubmit
- Multiple revision cycles allowed
- Support available for content creators

## Krawl Creation Guidelines

### Route Planning

1. **Logical Sequence**
   - Gems should connect in meaningful order
   - Consider walking distance between Gems
   - Plan efficient route

2. **Distance Considerations**
   - Total distance should be walkable
   - Consider average walking speed
   - Account for time at each Gem

3. **Duration Estimation**
   - Realistic time estimates
   - Include time for exploration
   - Consider breaks and rest stops

4. **Difficulty Assessment**
   - Easy: Short distance, flat terrain
   - Moderate: Medium distance, some elevation
   - Hard: Long distance, challenging terrain

### Krawl Quality Standards

- **Minimum 2 Gems:** At least 2 Gems required
- **All in Cebu City:** All Gems must be within boundaries
- **Meaningful Connection:** Gems should connect thematically or geographically
- **Clear Description:** Explain the trail's purpose and significance
- **Realistic Duration:** Accurate time estimates
- **Appropriate Difficulty:** Correct difficulty rating

### Route Optimization

- Use Mapbox Directions API for route calculation
- Consider traffic and accessibility
- Optimize for walking routes
- Provide route alternatives if applicable
